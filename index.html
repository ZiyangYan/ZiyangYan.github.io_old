The Electric Patrol UAV based on the M100 Platform
Z.Y. Yan*, J.X. Zhang*, J.D. Chen
(*indicates equal contribution)
Science And Technology
Paper

This paper introduces an electric inspection UAV based on the M100 aircraft platform to realize the flight control of the M100 UAV through the serial port communication. We used STM32 and A53 embedded system to position the dangerous objects on the transmission line through image processing. The UAV also has the GPS route planning and obstacle avoidance systems. After optimization, our UAV electric inspection method is carried out to solve the disadvantages of the traditional artificial power patrol line affected by the terrain, flood, landslide and other factors.

The Application Analysis of VHDL in Digital Integrated Circuit Design
Z.Y. Yan*, C.Y, Su**, H.X. Zhang*.
(*indicates equal contribution, **indicates corresponding author)
Automation & Instrumentation
Paper

With the development of digital electronic technology, the traditional digital circuit design method can no longer meet the requirements of modern digital integrated circuit system design, and the trend of completing hardware design with the help of hardware description language becomes an electronic design.Vhdl is now digital integrated circuit design application language, is an important part of modern electronic product design circuit gradually improve, this paper summarizes the connotation and characteristics of Vhdl, analyzes the basic interface design, multi-platform fusion, integrated operation, programming project integration, module tools centralization, advanced computing integration technology, actively expand the rationality of new technology in digital integrated circuit design overall integration, promote the development of modern new technology in China.

Method and system for positioning sphere in image
J.D. Chen, C.Y. Su, Z.Y. Yan, Z.B. Yao, J.X. Zhang, Y.Y. Chen, H.X. Zhang, Y.J. Shen, D.Y. Lin

CN201710173876.X
Patent

This patent proposes a sphere positioning method and system in an image through transfroms image to the color space and binarization and then extracts the contour of the connected domain in the resulting binary image. Then it takes advantage of geometric representation of the circle to exclude the non-circular contour. Finally, by taking the remaining contour to fit the ellipse, the corresponding contour representation in the sphere is found according to its eccentricity.

An Emergency Medical Rescue UAV System based on STM32
Z.Y. Yan, C.Y. Su*, J.D. Chen.
(*indicates corresponding author)
Digital Space
Paper

This paper introduces an intelligent air carrying system designed for emergency aid and emergency transportation according to the current medical market requirement which can solve the problems like transportation and terrain to deliver drugs to the destination in a short time. We also used complementary filter and Kalman filtering algorithm to realize the UAV attitude control and designed an automatic mission mode in this system.

The Design of A New Integrated FPV Fixed-wing Aircraft
Z.Y. Yan*, J.D. Chen*, H.X. Zhang.
(*indicates equal contribution)
Industry
Paper

With the continuous progress of UAV technology and the further expansion of the market, UAV is widely used in various fields. The new flying wing designed by us is based on flying wing X5. We integrate flying wing and Cessna to further increases the aspect ratio and vertical and horizontal stability. Our design increase the wind resistance, stability, mounting load and maximum range and also reduce the wing load that successfully fuse the advantages of flying wing and cessna. It is believed that our design will have a considerable prospects in the future FPV field.
